## 🎨 DESIGN SYSTEM

### Brutalist Principles
- **High Contrast** - Red, Yellow, Green, Cyan, Black, White
- **Chunky Borders** - 4-8px solid borders
- **Bold Typography** - Arial Black, Impact
- **All Uppercase** - Maximum impact
- **No Gradients** - Pure flat colors
- **Aggressive Shadows** - On hover effects
- **Function First** - But make it LOUD!

### Color Palette
| Color | Hex | Usage |
