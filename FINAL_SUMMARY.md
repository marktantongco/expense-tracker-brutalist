## 🎨 Design Philosophy

**Brutalist Web Design Principles:**
- Raw, unpolished aesthetics
- High contrast (Red, Yellow, Green, Black, White)
- Bold, aggressive typography (Arial Black, Impact)
- Chunky borders (4-8px solid)
- No gradients or shadows (except on hover)
- Function over form (but make it LOUD!)
- Maximum readability
